# North Island Animation - Frame by Frame Description

This document contains the frame-by-frame description of the 2D North Island animation workflow as defined in `north-island.html`.

### Frame 0: Highlight North Island

- **Map View:** Centered on New Zealand (-41.0, 174.0), Zoom level: 5.5.
- **Action:** Adds a pulsing, precise GeoJSON highlight layer over the North Island.

### Frame 1: North Island Overview

- **Map View:** Zooms in to focus specifically on the North Island (-38.8, 175.8), Zoom level: 7.
- **Action:** Removes the highlight layer. Adds main minimalist anchors: Auckland, Hamilton, Rotorua, Taupo, Waitomo, Tongariro, and Wellington.

### Frame 2: Auckland City Center

- **Map View:** Centered on Auckland CBD (-36.847, 174.765), Zoom level: 15.
- **Action:** Adds Auckland-specific anchors: Sky Tower, Queen Street, Art Gallery, and Queens Wharf.

### Frame 3: Auckland Culture

- **Map View:** Centered on Auckland culture hubs (-36.865, 174.750), Zoom level: 13.
- **Action:** Adds culture-focused anchors: Mount Eden, Auckland Zoo, and Museum.

### Frame 4: Auckland Coastal & Beyond

- **Map View:** Centered on the Hauraki Gulf and West Coast (-36.850, 175.050), Zoom level: 10.
- **Action:** Adds coastal anchors: Waiheke Island, Mission Bay, Rangitoto, Piha Beach, and Cathedral Cove.

### Frame 5: Central Region & Hamilton Gardens

- **Map View:** Centered on the Waikato/Bay of Plenty region (-37.95, 175.75), Zoom level: 9.
- **Action:** Displays a combined view of regional highlights including Hamilton, Hamilton Gardens, Waitomo Caves, Waitomo Adventure, Hobbiton, Blue Springs, and Rotorua.

### Frame 6: Rotorua Villages

- **Map View:** Centered on Rotorua's cultural villages (-38.18, 176.27), Zoom level: 11.
- **Action:** Adds anchors for City Center, Whakarewarewa, Te Puia, Mitai, and Te Pā Tū.

### Frame 7: Rotorua Spas

- **Map View:** Centered on Rotorua's thermal spa region (-38.10, 176.30), Zoom level: 11.5.
- **Action:** Adds anchors for City Center, Hell's Gate, Polynesian Spa, and Wai Ariki Spa.

### Frame 8: Rotorua Adventure

- **Map View:** Centered on Rotorua's adventure hubs (-38.13, 176.25), Zoom level: 12.5.
- **Action:** Adds anchors for City Center, Redwoods, Skyline Rotorua, and Taiao Adventure.

### Frame 9: Thermal Highway

- **Map View:** Centered on the road between Rotorua and Taupo (-38.45, 176.25), Zoom level: 9.
- **Action:** Adds anchors for Rotorua, Taupo, Wai-O-Tapu, and Waimangu.

### Frame 10: Taupo Region

- **Map View:** Centered on Lake Taupo (-38.68, 176.05), Zoom level: 11.5.
- **Action:** Adds anchors for City Center, Huka Falls, Taupo McDonald's, and Maori Rock Carvings.

### Frame 11: Tongariro National Park

- **Map View:** Centered on Tongariro (-39.15, 175.63), Zoom level: 10.5.
- **Action:** Displays the main Tongariro National Park anchor.

### Frame 12: Tongariro Local

- **Map View:** Centered on the Tongariro area (-39.08, 175.60), Zoom level: 10.
- **Action:** Adds anchors for Tongariro, Tūrangi, and Waimarino.

### Frame 13: Transit South

- **Map View:** Wide view of the southern transit corridor (-40.25, 175.40), Zoom level: 7.5.
- **Action:** Adds anchors for Tongariro, Wellington, Taihape, Bulls, and Palmerston North.

### Frame 14: Wellington City

- **Map View:** Centered on Wellington (-41.285, 174.775), Zoom level: 13.5.
- **Action:** Adds Wellington anchors: City Center, Cable Car, Zealandia, and Te Papa Museum.
